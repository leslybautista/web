Global Political Systems

This visualization categorizes countries into four regime types: Liberal Democracy, Electoral Democracy, Electoral Autocracy, and Closed Autocracy. The data reveals a polarized world where some regions strengthen democratic institutions while others experience democratic backsliding and autocratic consolidation. This map captures the current state of global governance and the ongoing struggle between democracy and authoritarianism.

Global Democratic Trends

The world has recently become less democratic. Democracy is in decline across multiple metrics, whether we look at changes in the number of democracies, democratic rights, or countries experiencing autocratization.
